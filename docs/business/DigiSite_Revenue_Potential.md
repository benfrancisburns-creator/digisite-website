## How DigiSite Generates Revenue

DigiSite screens generate advertising revenue every fortnight across six premium advertiser slots per screen. Both DigiScaff and DigiPerim operate on the same commercial model used by Clear Channel, JCDecaux, and Bauer Media Outdoor.

| | |
|---|---|
| **6 slots per screen** | Up to 6 advertisers share each screen in a rotating loop |
| **10 seconds per slot** | Each advertiser plays once per 60-second loop, all day |
| **Fortnightly booking cycle** | UK OOH industry standard — same as all major operators |

## DigiScaff — Large Format Digital Billboard

DigiScaff is positioned as large format digital billboard inventory — comparable to a digital 48-sheet or 96-sheet roadside screen. The standard unit measures **8.64m × 3.84m — 33m² of 9,000-nit LED** on a city centre scaffold face. Unlike a fixed billboard, DigiScaff occupies prime mid-street positions on active development sites that permanent structures cannot reach.

## DigiPerim — Premium Digital Perimeter Screens

DigiPerim screens are integrated into the site perimeter hoarding at eye level — directly in the path of pedestrians passing the site. Each individual panel measures **2.88m × 1.92m**, larger than a standard digital 6-sheet, positioned in a captive audience environment that static street furniture cannot replicate.

Critically, DigiPerim is not a single screen — **it is a run of inventory.** A standard 15-metre perimeter frontage accommodates five DigiPerim panels side by side, each carrying six advertiser slots independently. The result is a digital advertising strip running the full length of the site boundary.

## The Combined Site — One Partnership, Two Revenue Streams

Every construction site in the DigiSite network is assessed for both products. Where both are viable — which is the case on the majority of city centre developments — DigiScaff runs on the scaffold face and DigiPerim runs along the perimeter hoarding simultaneously. One partner relationship. Two independent revenue streams. Infrastructure that was previously generating nothing.
